Eddy Zulkifly, a Data Engineer at Kinaxis, brings a unique perspective to data engineering.  His background in physical warehouse optimization informs his current work in digital data optimization.  Eddy highlights the critical role of understanding data warehousing fundamentals in both physical and digital contexts. 

The transition from business analytics to data engineering demands new tools and a shift in mindset. Eddy focuses on FinOps,  applying process optimization techniques learned in his supply chain past. He sees FinOps as a digital evolution of efficiency principles. Eddy leverages tools like Docker, Terraform, and data tagging to manage cloud costs.  He works collaboratively with diverse teams to build data-driven solutions that optimize resource allocation.  Eddy emphasizes continuous learning and community engagement as crucial components of success in the evolving data landscape.
